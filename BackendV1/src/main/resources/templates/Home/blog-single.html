#2. Daily Doctor's Story

On the occasion of International Children's Day 1/6 and Action Month for Children, Bac Giang Health Sector Trade Unions organized many activities to bring children a warm and meaningful Children's Day..

With the desire to bring joy to the children being treated at the Hospital, the Executive Committee (BCH) of the Hospital's Obstetrics and Gynecology Hospital has developed a plan to organize the program "Happy Children's New Year 1/6/2022". . 350 gifts were given directly by the doctors and nurses to the pediatric patients being treated here. The gifts, though small, are a great sharing and encouragement for children and their families, helping them have more joy and excitement on Children's Day, thereby bringing effective treatment. positive. Also on this occasion, the Executive Board of the Hospital's Trade Union organized to give 700 gifts, each worth 100,000 VND, to the children of cadres, officials and employees in the unit aged 15 years and under.

In addition, the hospital also received the attention and sharing of units and sponsors for pediatric patients, especially children with difficult circumstances. On May 31, the Women's Union, Youth Union, Logistics Department, Professional Records Department - Provincial Police distributed more than 500 free porridge meals to patients and patients' family members who are being treated at the hospital. and gave 16 gifts to 16 disadvantaged pediatric patients being treated at the Intensive Care Units, General Pediatrics, and Respiratory Departments. Mr. Than Quoc Thuat (in Tang Tien commune, Viet Yen district) gave 16 gifts to poor children.

On the morning of June 1, the Union of Rehabilitation Hospital cooperated with Phuong Hoang Truc Lam Zen Monastery (Yen Dung) to organize the program "Happy Children's Day 1/6" with many activities and meaningful gifts for children. for 50 pediatric patients being treated at the hospital. Here, the children can participate in many games, many fun and useful cultural and artistic activities.

Annually, the Rehabilitation Hospital receives and treats more than 3,000 pediatric patients. In addition to focusing on improving the quality of medical examination and treatment for children with disabilities, Bac Giang Rehabilitation Hospital also cares about the physical and spiritual lives of the children. Nguyen Thi Thoi - Director of the Rehabilitation Hospital said: "For children who are sick and are being treated at the hospital, a complete and complete Children's Day is what they always dream of. . With the desire to encourage and share, and at the same time ease the mental and physical pain of the children, every year the Party Committee, the hospital's Board of Directors direct the Trade Union to coordinate with the mass organizations in the unit. deploying many activities of practical significance to create a comfortable and joyful atmosphere for children, especially underprivileged pediatric patients due to motor retardation, cerebral palsy, autism….”
